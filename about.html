About Chess Rating Tools

What this is

Free, unofficial calculators for estimating chess rating changes under FIDE and CFC regulations. Enter your current rating, add game results, and see your projected new rating instantly — no sign-up, no account, no data stored.

Accuracy

Calculations follow the official FIDE Rating Regulations (effective March 2024) and CFC Rating Regulations. The FIDE calculator includes the 2650+ Classical exception (October 2025) and the faster time-control amendment (January 2026).

Results are estimates. Official ratings are calculated by FIDE and CFC from full tournament data submitted by arbiters — this tool cannot account for provisional ratings, initial rating establishment, or performance rating norms.

⚠️ Unofficial tool. Always verify your official rating at ratings.fide.com or chess.ca.
