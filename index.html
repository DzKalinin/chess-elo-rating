About FIDE 2026 Rating Regulations

This calculator follows the official FIDE Handbook Section 4. In 2026, regulations emphasize accuracy for elite players and specific handling of significant rating gaps.

The 2650+ Classical Exception

For players with a rating of 2650 or higher in classical chess, the standard 400-point rating difference cap is removed. This ensure that elite competition is reflected accurately in the Elo system without artificial point compression.

How Elo Change is Calculated

The formula uses the probability of winning based on the rating difference between two players. The change (Δ) is determined by multiplying the K-factor by the difference between the actual result and the expected score.
